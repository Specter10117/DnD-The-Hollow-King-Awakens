# Player, Behavior, Game Behavior & DM Expectations
### Alcohol, Drugs, Food:
- I am fine with it, but if it becomes a problem I will ask that you all stop.
### Cellphones & Other Devices:
- As long as it is not distracting you and especially others I am ok with it.
### Player Attention:
- Every time I have to try and get your attention, I will subtract EXP from your progression.
	- I am ok with it if it's an emergency, or something out of your control.

### Disruptive Topics:
Are real life topics like religion, politics, porn, etc okay at the table?
- If you want to talk about them before the game starts that is ay ok
- During the campaign I will allow the occasional mention if its related to what is happening in the campaign. (Limited to 1 minute)
- After the session go right ahead.
- Keep things Twitch and YouTube Safe
	- These Games will be Streamed Live

### Unannounced Dice Rolls:
- Do ***NOT*** Roll Unannounced!!!
	- If you Roll then tell me what you want to do, I WILL make you reroll.
	- I NEED to know what you want to do and what you expect for an outcome.
		- I then will tell you if it is possible and what to roll.

### Dice Rolls Against Other PCs:
- What do the players want?
	- No but depends on the situation.

### PVP:
- I will only allow PVP in Social Interactions, they can not lead to the death of another PC, the lowest your HP will go is 1 during such encounters. 
	- These will only be allowed RIGHT before a Long Rest.
- Do the players want PVP?
	- situational.
- Accidental PVP?
	- It is fine.

### PC Secrets:
- Yes PCs can have secrets from the party.
	- Secrets can not be about harming or wanting to kill a PC or a PCs Friend/Family.

### Player Expectations:
- Toxi: Have fun, hang out with friends, roleplay, a little bit of combat, avoiding pc deaths, fun loot.
- Shadow: Have fun, roleplay, no min maxing, love loot, like dice rolls.
- Winter: Roleplay, Combat, Chilling with Friends

### Player Discomfort:
- What are players not comfortable with?
	- Toxi: No IRL Racism, No IRL Hate Speech, No Animal Abuse, No descriptive Sex/R**e. No Beastality.
	- Shadow: No IRL Racism, No IRL Hate Speech, No Animal Abuse, No descriptive Sex/R**e. No Beastality.
	- Winter: No IRL Racism, No IRL Hate Speech, No Animal Abuse, No descriptive Sex/R**e. No Beastality.

### Game Balance:
- Game is gonna be fairly balanced, with some unbalanced encounters.

### Rule Debates:
- If a ruling is in question, we will pause for a max of 2-5 Minutes to look it up.
- If no answer can be found in those 2 minutes, the DM decides the ruling in that moment.
- After the session if it is found out to be another way, I the DM will address it before the next session and will use the ruling from there on out.

### Meta Gaming:
- If you know something but your character does not. YOU CAN NOT ACT ON YOUR KNOWLEDGE.
- If you know something that your character MIGHT know. RP why your character knows it.
	- Example: I had a dream and remembered this guy said this!
- If you know something and your character definitely knows. No RP is required unless the player wants.
- If you do not know something and your Character does. I the DM will let you know when it comes up.

### Min Maxxing:
- Go ahead
	-  Do not let it become an issue though
	- If I am not having fun with your character, I will start putting restrictions on you or make you respec your whole sheet.

### Murder Hobos:
- This campaign is Evil in our eyes.
- You will kill other humans that you as a player will see as innocent but your character sees as the enemy.

### Behavior Rules:
- Respect one another
- You all will get your time in the spotlight


# The DM

### DM Style:
- I am a RAW and a RAI type of DM
	- If the rule clearly state one way the rule is as such
	- If the rule does not clearly state one way, I the DM will rule how it goes and make note of that ruling.
- I do prepare the campaign but Improve is welcome.
- I do homebrew
- I would prefer a Roleplay type of Campaign

### Player Absences:
- How many players need to be absent before rescheduling?
	- If 1 is missing Reschedule, but if a player says to play without them, give a reason why they aren't there.

### Player Narrative Authority:
- You the player have a lot of narrative authority, but may be restricted if what you want to do interferes with the progression.
	- If it advances your character progression but not the story that is fine.
		- Like if you wanted to find a magic item to become a god, you and the group can decide to go hunting for it.

### Pet Peeves:
- No unannounced rolls
- Please be ready on your turn
	- If you are consistently having your turns be longer than the other players, I will start timing you.

# General Rules

### Healing:
- Can be a bonus action or an Action (Players Choice)

### Downtime/Crafting:
- If you want to train stats or do crafting of something during downtime, please discuss this with the DM.

### Party Rolls:
- When asked you all or a specific use will roll a stat I the DM ask.
	- I will let players know if they can provide the help action to the roll or if they also can roll.

### Gaining Inspiration:
- You gain inspiration if you impress me with how you handle a situation.
- You may also gain Inspiration if you decide to take a terrible fate if asked on a failed save.
- You can only have 1 inspiration, if you gain another you must give it to another player or lose it.
